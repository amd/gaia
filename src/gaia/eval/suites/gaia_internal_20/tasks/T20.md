# Task

The fixture at ``fixtures/T20.patch`` injects a mock history of yesterday's
session into ``$SANDBOX/.gaia/coder/history-mock.jsonl``: three completed
tasks (one PR opened, one learnings_log entry, one failed self-fix) plus
five EM-inbox exchanges.

Produce a **standup report** in ``$SANDBOX/.eval-artifacts/T20/standup.md``
summarising yesterday's work. The standup must include:

1. **Done today** — bullet per completed task with a 1-line "why".
2. **In progress** — any paused tasks (none in the mock history — write
   "None" rather than omitting the section).
3. **Blocked / needs EM input** — any EM-inbox items marked ``pending``.
4. **Learnings** — tasks added to ``learnings.log``.
5. **Red flags** — failed self-fix PRs or guardrail trips.

## Style guidance

- Tight. No preamble. No "as your AI agent, I am pleased to report…".
- Cite specific task/feedback/PR IDs.
- If a section is empty, say so explicitly.

## Acceptance

- ``standup.md`` exists and is non-empty.
- The five sections listed above are present (``grep -c "^## " standup.md``
  should be 5).
- ``python util/lint.py --all`` unaffected (no Python changes).

## Budget

Diff ≤ 200 LoC, ≤ 20 min wall clock.
