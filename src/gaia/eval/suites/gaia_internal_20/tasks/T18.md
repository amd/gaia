# Task

The fixture at ``fixtures/T18.patch`` seeds an ``except Exception: pass``
block in ``src/gaia/coder/introspect/__init__.py`` — a direct violation
of the CLAUDE.md "No Silent Fallbacks — Fail Loudly" rule.

## Required work

1. Replace the `except Exception: pass` with a narrow catch that either:
   - catches the specific exception (e.g. `FileNotFoundError`) and
     re-raises with context (`raise RuntimeError(...) from e`), OR
   - catches it, logs a warning via `gaia.logger.get_logger(__name__)`,
     and re-raises.
2. The seeded test ``tests/unit/test_t18_seeded.py`` asserts that a
   known trigger (reading a non-existent path) surfaces an exception
   with actionable context in its message.
3. Cite the rule in the PR body: "Per CLAUDE.md 'No Silent Fallbacks' —
   actionable errors name *what failed*, *what the caller should do*,
   *where to look next*."

## Acceptance

- ``pytest tests/unit/test_t18_seeded.py -x`` passes.
- No other tests regress.
- Diff ≤ 15 LoC.

## Budget

Diff ≤ 15 LoC, ≤ 10 min wall clock. This is the simplest task in the
suite and the canonical smoke-test candidate.
